# Design Tokens

## Brand Colors
- **Shop (Carvantooo):** `#FFB300` (Orange)
- **Werkstatt / Autohandel (OpenCarBox):** `#FFA800` (Orange-Amber)

## Dual-Brand Rules
- Logos only on black background.
- Shop primary: `#FFB300`.
- Werkstatt/Autohandel primary: `#FFA800`.
- 8px Grid system must be maintained.
- Max 3 CTA types.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: `375px`
- Tablet: `768px`
- Desktop: `1024px`
- Large Desktop: `1280px`
- Extra Large Desktop: `1440px`
